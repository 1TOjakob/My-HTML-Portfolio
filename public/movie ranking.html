My top 3

King Arthur : Legend of the Sword

Robbed of his birthright, Arthur comes up the hard way in the back alleys of the city.
But once he pulls the sword from the stone, he is forced to acknowledge his true legacy.

Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse

Miles Morales catapults across the Multiverse, where he encounters
a team of Spider-People charged with protecting its very existence.
When the heroes clash on how to handle a new threat, Miles must redefine what it means to be a hero.

Avengers: Endgame

After the devastating events of Avengers: Infinity War (2018), the universe is in ruins.
With the help of remaining allies, the Avengers assemble once more in order to reverse Thanos' actions
and restore balance to the universe.
